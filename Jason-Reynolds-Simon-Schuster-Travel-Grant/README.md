# Jason Reynolds / Simon & Schuster Travel Grant

**Status:** ⚠️ LIMITED RELEVANCE — For librarians, not authors
**Application Cycle:** September 2025 (annual)
**Organization:** American Library Association (ALA)
**Website:** https://www.ala.org/news/2025/09/2026-jason-reynolds-simon-schuster-travel-grant-application-now-open

---

## Grant Overview

**Program:** Jason Reynolds / Simon & Schuster Travel Grant
**Funding:** Covers travel expenses for ALA events
**Number of Grants:** Five (5) grants
**Recipients:** Black/African American youth or teen services librarians

---

## Eligibility

**Who Can Apply:**
- Public or school library workers
- Youth or teen services librarians/media specialists
- Black/African American library professionals

**Who Cannot Apply:**
- Authors (this grant is for librarians, not creators)
