## Fellowship Overview

**Program:** Teachers Going Overseas Fellowship
**Duration:** Yearlong fellowship
**Travel:** 2-3 week international trip to TGC-selected location
**Focus:** Professional development for K-12 teachers

---

## What Fellowship Offers

**Coverage:**
- **Airfare** to international destination
- **Hotel accommodations**
- **Travel incidentals**
- **Free online training course**
- **Trip to Washington, D.C. for Global Education Symposium**

**Requirements:**
- Full-time K-12 teachers with at least 3 years experience
- 50%+ of time in classroom instruction
- Commitment to share learning with students and colleagues

---

## Alexandria's World Connection

**Why It's LIMITED:**
- **Educator-specific:** For current K-12 teachers, not independent authors or consultants
- **Employment requirement:** Must be full-time classroom teacher
- **Return commitment:** Must return to teaching after trip

**Indirect Value:**
- If Marie were to return to classroom teaching, could travel and develop Alexandria's World materials based on experience
- Could partner with teacher who receives fellowship, incorporating Alexandria's World concepts
- Provides model for international cultural immersion for educators
