## Grant Overview

**Program:** Renaissance Project Research Grants
**Organization:** Chicago Public Library Foundation
**Funding:** $4,000 per grant (10 grants total)
**Focus:** Short-term research in African American archival collections

---

## What Grants Support

**Eligible Research:**
- Research-related costs for new scholarship on Black history and culture
- Access to Chicago Public Library's African American archival collections
- Projects connecting to African American historical and cultural themes

**Covers:**
- Research travel expenses
- Materials and documentation
- Access fees
- Research-related costs

---

## Eligibility

**Requirements:**
- Projects must produce new scholarship on Black history and culture
- Research must utilize African American archival collections
- Clear research question and scholarly methodology

**Open To:**
- Researchers, scholars, authors, educators
- Not explicitly limited to Chicago residents

## Application Requirements

**Materials Needed:**
- Research proposal with clear question and methodology
- Timeline for research project
- Budget showing how $4,000 will be used
- Description of how research will contribute to new scholarship
- CV or professional credentials

**Competition:**
- 10 grants of $4,000 each
- Competitive review process
- Focus on scholarly merit and contribution to Black history and culture
