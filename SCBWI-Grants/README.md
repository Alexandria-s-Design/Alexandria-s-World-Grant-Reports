## Application Requirements

### Work-In-Progress Award (March 31, 2026)

**Requirements:**
- SCBWI membership
- Manuscript not under contract
- Word count/genre adherence (check specific category requirements)
- Submission fee (typically $25-50 for SCBWI awards)

**Materials Needed:**
- Complete manuscript or substantial sample
- Project description and summary
- Statement about work's significance and goals
- Author bio and credentials

### General SCBWI Application Tips

- Follow submission guidelines precisely (different grants have different requirements)
- Demonstrate understanding of children's book market
- Show how project fits SCBWI's mission
- Highlight professional development and publishing goals
- Proofread carefully (professional presentation matters)
