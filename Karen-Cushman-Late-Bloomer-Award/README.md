## Award Overview

**Program:** Karen Cushman Late Bloomer Award
**Funding:** $500 + free tuition to any SCBWI conference worldwide
**Focus:** Supporting unpublished children's authors/illustrators over 50

---

## What Award Offers

- **$500 cash award**
- **Free tuition** to any SCBWI conference globally (Winter, Summer, regional)
- Recognition for "late-blooming" authors who found their calling later in life

---

## Eligibility

**Requirements:**
- Unpublished children's author or illustrator
- Age 50 or older
- SCBWI membership required

**Focus:**
- Celebrates authors who began writing/illustrating for children later in life
- Honors persistence and dedication to craft
