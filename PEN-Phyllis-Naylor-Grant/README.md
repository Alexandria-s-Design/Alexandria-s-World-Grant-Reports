## Grant Overview

**Program:** PEN/Phyllis Naylor Grant
**Funding:** $5,000
**Focus:** Supporting completion of a novel-in-progress
**Organization:** PEN America

---

## Eligibility

**Requirements:**
- U.S.-published authors
- Previously published children's or YA novel(s) with critical acclaim but low commercial sales
- Novel-in-progress not published before April 1, 2025

**Exclusions:**
- Instructional or scholarly writing
- Genre fiction (unless literary quality is emphasized)

---

## Alexandria's World Connection

**Why It's a Potential Fit:**
- $5,000 could support illustration or manuscript development for Alexandria's World
- Supports "critically acclaimed but low-selling" authors
- Focus on literary merit

**Challenges:**
- Alexandria's World is picture book format (not novel)
- Requires previous publication in children's/YA
- Focus on novels, not picture books

**Alternative Approach:**
- If Marie publishes a YA or middle-grade novel based on Alexandria's World concept
- Could apply for $5,000 to support that project

---

## Application Requirements

**Materials Needed:**
- Manuscript sample or full manuscript
- Proof of previous publication
- Sales figures (to demonstrate "low-selling" status)
- Critical reviews or acclaim documentation
- Project description and timeline

**Submission:** Ongoing (check PEN America website for current cycle)
